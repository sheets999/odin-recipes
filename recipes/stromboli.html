How to make the best Stromboli

My recipe for the best stromboli.

  • Spicy sausage
  • Pepperoni
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Loaf of bread
  1. Brown the sausage in a pan
  2. Roll the bread out thin
  3. Fill the bread with all the other ingredients
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes
