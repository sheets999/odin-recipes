Tacos

How to make some good Tacos!

This is a recipe for Tacos that was found randomly on the internet.

  • 1 Chorizo sausage link
  • 2 Tablespoons chiptole peppers
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 Tablespoons chopped onion
  1. Mix the chorizo, pepper, and onions in a skillet
  2. Cook until the meat is brown
  3. Fill the corn tortillas with filling and enjoy!
